What You’ll Be Doing:
* Study and summarize existing machine learning algorithms and frameworks used within the team and company.

* Dynamically refine predictions as more information becomes available during the simulation process.
* Summarize findings and present research achievements to the team.
What You’ll Need:
* Currently enrolled in a full-time degree program at a Canadian university.
* Pursuing a Master’s or PhD in Electrical Engineering, Computational Science, Computer Science, Software Engineering, or Physics.
* Familiarity with machine learning and prior experience with ML-related projects.
* Proficiency in programming languages such as Python (strongly preferred) and C++.
* Understanding of the physics of electromagnetics.
